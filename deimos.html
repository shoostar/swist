PIF–813 DEIMOS

BROADSIDE DOMINATION

Ship number 813 'Deimos' is a Watcher-class battlecarrier capable of plantery landings and orbital subjugation. Built for Pyrrhus' Imperial Fleet in 2216, 'Deimos' first cut her teeth in battle at the Fall of Masperon.

The most prominent feature of 'Deimos'' hull is its asymmetrical broadside design. Flanking a ship with its massive starboard shear, the 36 plasma cannons, 4 rocket pods, 4 flax cannons, and a whopping 18 160mm artillery turrets absolutely shred anything in their line of sight. While certainly not the most nimble of ships, the widened firing arc allows 'Deimos' to stay her course without having to correct its pitch or roll to compensate for poor firing position.
